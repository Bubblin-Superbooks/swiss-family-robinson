'My dear wife!' I exclaimed. 'This is beautiful! You have done wonders! Did you not find the work too hard?'

'The ground is light and easy to dig hereabouts,' she replied. 'I have planted potatoes, and cassava-roots, there is space for sugar-canes, and the young fruit trees, and I shall want you to contrive to irrigate them, by leading water from the cascades in hollow bamboos. Up by the sheltering rocks I mean to have pineapples* and melons, they will look splendid when they spread there.
